Data Management and Privacy Settings

This document outlines the settings available for managing your data and privacy preferences.

Data Export

Download a copy of your data.

How to use: Click "Export Data" to initiate the process of downloading all your account data. This is useful for backup purposes or if you're considering account deletion.

Account Deletion

Permanently delete your account and all associated data.

How to use: Click "Delete Account" to begin the account deletion process. This action is irreversible, so make sure to export any important data before proceeding.

Data Retention

Set how long we keep your data.

Current setting: 1 year

How to use: Choose the duration for which you want the service to retain your data. After this period, data will be automatically deleted.

GDPR Compliance

Opt-in to GDPR compliant data processing.

How to use: Enable this option to ensure your data is processed in compliance with GDPR regulations. This is particularly important for users in the European Union.

Billing Cycle

Choose your preferred billing cycle.

Current setting: Monthly

How to use: Select your preferred billing frequency. This affects when and how often you're charged for the service.

Integrations

Manage your connected third-party services.

How to use: Click "Manage Integrations" to view, add, or remove connections to other services. Be mindful of data sharing when connecting third-party services.

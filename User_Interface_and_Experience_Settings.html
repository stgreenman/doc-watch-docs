User Interface and Experience Settings

This document outlines the settings available for customizing your user interface and overall experience.

Dark Mode

Toggle dark mode for the application.

How to use: Enable this feature to switch the application to a darker color scheme, which can be easier on the eyes in low-light environments.

Default Dashboard

Choose your default view when logging in.

Current setting: Overview

How to use: Select which dashboard or page you'd like to see first when you log into the application. This can help streamline your workflow by taking you directly to the most relevant information.

Company Data

Manage your company's information and settings.

How to use: Access this section to update or modify information specific to your company's account. This may include things like company name, address, or other organizational details.

Switch to Chat

Quick access to the chat interface.

How to use: Use this option to quickly switch from the settings page to the chat interface, where you can communicate with support or other users (depending on the application's features).

Admin Dashboard

Access advanced administrative controls.

How to use: If you have administrative privileges, use this to access additional settings and controls for managing users, permissions, and other high-level account features.

Note: Some options may only be visible or accessible depending on your account type or permissions level. If you don't see a particular setting, you may need to contact your account administrator or our support team for assistance.

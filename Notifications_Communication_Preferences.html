Notifications and Communication Preferences

This document outlines the settings available for managing your notifications and communication preferences.

Email Notifications

Choose what types of emails you'd like to receive.

Options:

  • Marketing emails
  • Product updates
  • Security alerts

How to use: Toggle each option on or off based on your preferences. Keeping security alerts on is recommended for account safety.

Language Preference

Select your preferred language for the application.

Current setting: English

How to use: Choose your preferred language from the dropdown menu. This will change the language of the user interface and communications.

Time Zone

Set your local time zone.

Current setting: UTC

How to use: Select your local time zone to ensure that all time-sensitive information and notifications are displayed correctly.

Desktop Notifications

Receive notifications on your desktop.

How to use: Enable this feature to receive real-time notifications on your desktop for important updates or alerts, even when you're not actively using the application.

Beta Features

Opt-in to try new beta features.

How to use: Enable this option to get early access to new features before they're officially released. Note that beta features may be less stable than regular features.
